🎤 VoiceKeyboard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: VoiceKeyboard processes your voice 100% on your device. No audio, text, or personal data is ever sent to any server or third party. We collect nothing.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever.

VoiceKeyboard does not have accounts, logins, analytics, advertising identifiers, crash reporting services, or any network calls that transmit user data.

2. Microphone Access

VoiceKeyboard requests access to your microphone solely to capture your voice when you activate the recording hotkey. Audio is:

  • Captured in memory only while you are actively recording.
  • Processed immediately on your device using a local AI model (OpenAI Whisper, running fully offline).
  • Never written to disk (beyond the temporary in-memory buffer).
  • Never transmitted — no internet connection is used during transcription.
  • Discarded from memory as soon as transcription completes.

3. AI Model Downloads

When you choose to download a Whisper transcription model (e.g., Base or Small), the app fetches the model file from Hugging Face's public servers (huggingface.co). This download:

  • Is initiated only by your explicit action in the Settings screen.
  • Downloads only the model weights file — no personal data is sent.
  • Stores the model in %APPDATA%\VoiceKeyboard\models\ on your device.

After download the model runs entirely offline. No further network communication occurs.

4. Local Storage

VoiceKeyboard stores the following files on your device only:

  • %APPDATA%\VoiceKeyboard\settings.json — your preferences (hotkey, model choice, etc.)
  • %APPDATA%\VoiceKeyboard\models\ — downloaded AI model files
  • %APPDATA%\VoiceKeyboard\crash.log — error log (local only, never uploaded)

None of this data leaves your device.

5. Clipboard

When transcription is complete, VoiceKeyboard briefly places the transcribed text on your clipboard to facilitate pasting. The previous clipboard content is not stored or transmitted. You can re-paste the transcribed text with Ctrl+V.

6. Third-Party SDKs

VoiceKeyboard uses the following open-source libraries, none of which collect data:

  • Whisper.net — .NET wrapper for OpenAI's Whisper model (MIT License)
  • NAudio — audio capture library (MIT License)

7. Children's Privacy

VoiceKeyboard does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will change. Given that we collect no data, meaningful changes to this policy are unlikely.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at: privacy@voicekeyboard.app
